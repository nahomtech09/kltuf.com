Refund Policy

We want every buyer to be satisfied. Here is our return and refund policy:

  • Item not as described: Contact us within 3 days of receiving your item. If the item significantly differs from its description, we will issue a full refund once the item is returned.

To request a refund or return, use the Contact form with your order details.

Refunds are issued via the same payment method used for purchase (PayPal or Zelle) within 3 business days of approval.
