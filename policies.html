Privacy Policy

KLTUF Store ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Contact & inquiry submissions — name, email, and message when you contact us.
  • Seller submissions — name, contact info, payment details (Zelle/PayPal/Venmo handle), item details, and photos submitted through our Sell With Us form.
  • Payment processing — card payments and Apple Pay are processed by Stripe. PayPal payments are processed by PayPal. Zelle and Venmo transfers are handled directly by those platforms. We never see, store, or have access to your full card number or banking credentials.
  • Browser storage — your shopping cart is stored locally in your browser (localStorage). We do not store cart data on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and complete transactions.
  • To review, verify, and list items submitted by sellers.
  • To coordinate payouts to sellers after a sale.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • PayPal — payment processing. Subject to PayPal's Privacy Policy.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels. No personal browsing data is collected or shared.

Data Retention

Seller submission data is retained for the duration of the listing and up to 90 days after the item is sold or removed. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing freta@kltuf.com.

Refund Policy

We want every buyer to be satisfied with their purchase. Please read our return and refund policy carefully before buying.

Eligible Refunds

  • Item not as described: Contact us within 3 days of receiving your item. If the item significantly differs from its description or photos, we will issue a full refund once the item is returned in its original condition.
  • Item not received: If your item has not arrived within the estimated delivery window, contact us and we will investigate with the carrier and resolve the issue promptly.
  • Damaged in transit: Contact us with photos within 48 hours of delivery. We will work with you on a full or partial refund depending on the extent of damage.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind — all sales are final unless the item is not as described.
  • Items returned without prior approval.
  • Damage caused by the buyer after receipt.

How to Request a Refund

Email freta@kltuf.com with your name, the item purchased, date of purchase, and reason for the refund request. Include photos if applicable.

Approved refunds are issued via the same payment method used for purchase (Stripe/PayPal/Zelle/Venmo) within 3 business days of approval.

Shipping Policy

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days of confirmed payment. You will receive a tracking number by email once your item ships.

Shipping Methods & Carriers

  • We ship via USPS, UPS, or FedEx depending on item size and weight.
  • Estimated delivery: 3–7 business days after shipment (continental US).
  • Expedited shipping may be available — contact us before purchasing to arrange.

Shipping Costs

  • Shipping costs are calculated based on item weight, dimensions, and destination.
  • Free shipping on orders over $75 (continental US only).
  • Buyer pays shipping on orders under $75 unless otherwise stated in the listing.

Local Pickup

Local pickup is available for select items. If a listing offers local pickup, you will coordinate directly with us via email after purchase.

Lost or Delayed Packages

If your package is delayed beyond the estimated window or shows as lost by the carrier, contact us at freta@kltuf.com. We will file a carrier claim and issue a replacement or refund if the package is confirmed lost.

International Shipping

We currently ship within the continental United States only. International shipping is not available at this time.

Seller Agreement

By submitting an item through the KLTUF Sell With Us form, you ("Seller") agree to the following terms with KLTUF Store ("KLTUF").

Commission Structure

  • • Sale price $1–$50: Seller receives 65% — KLTUF retains 35%
  • • Sale price $51–$200: Seller receives 70% — KLTUF retains 30%
  • • Sale price $200+: Seller receives 75% — KLTUF retains 25%

Commission is calculated on the final sale price, excluding any shipping costs charged to the buyer.

Verification Process

  • All submitted items are subject to verification by KLTUF before being listed.
  • You must provide clear, accurate photos from multiple angles (front, back, sides, any defects).
  • KLTUF may request additional photos or a short video before approving a listing.
  • Items that are misrepresented, prohibited, or fail verification will not be listed. You will be notified by email or phone.
  • Verification is typically completed within 24 hours of submission.

Seller Obligations

  • You confirm that you are the rightful owner of the item and have the right to sell it.
  • You confirm that all information and photos submitted are truthful and accurate.
  • You agree to ship or deliver the item promptly once a sale is confirmed by KLTUF.
  • You agree not to sell the same item through another channel once it is listed on KLTUF.

Payout Timeline

  • Seller payout is issued within 3 business days after the buyer confirms receipt of the item.
  • Payouts are made via Zelle, PayPal, or Venmo — whichever method you selected on your submission form.
  • You are responsible for ensuring your payment information is accurate. KLTUF is not liable for failed transfers due to incorrect payment details.

Unsold Items

  • If your item has not sold after 30 days, KLTUF will contact you to discuss a price adjustment or removal of the listing.
  • No fees are charged on unsold items.
  • You may request removal of your listing at any time by contacting freta@kltuf.com.

Item Loss or Damage

  • KLTUF acts as a listing intermediary and does not take physical possession of items unless a local drop-off arrangement is made.
  • For shipped items, the seller is responsible for proper packaging. KLTUF will assist in filing carrier claims if damage occurs during transit.
  • If an item is lost or damaged after a local drop-off to KLTUF, we will work with you in good faith to reach a fair resolution.

KLTUF reserves the right to remove or decline any listing at any time, without obligation to provide a reason, if the item violates our prohibited items policy, fails verification, or is otherwise unsuitable for the marketplace.
