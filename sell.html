Questions

FAQ

How long does it take to sell my item? ▼
It depends on the item and price. Most items sell within 1–2 weeks. Electronics and popular brands typically sell faster. We'll keep you updated.
Do I need to ship the item? ▼
Once your item sells, we'll coordinate with you. You can either ship it directly to the buyer (we provide the label) or drop it off locally.
What items do you accept? ▼
We accept most items including electronics, toys, furniture, clothing, appliances, tools, and more. We reserve the right to decline items that are damaged beyond use, counterfeit, or prohibited.
When and how do I get paid? ▼
You get paid within 3 business days after the buyer confirms receipt. We send payment via Zelle, PayPal, or Venmo — whichever you choose when submitting.
Can I set my own price? ▼
Yes! You submit your asking price and we'll review it. If we think a different price will sell faster, we'll discuss it with you before listing. You always have final say.
What if my item doesn't sell? ▼
If your item hasn't sold after 30 days, we'll reach out to discuss lowering the price or returning the item to you. No fees are ever charged for unsold items.
