Questions

FAQ

What does the verification process involve? ▼
We review your submitted photos and description within 24 hours. If the item looks good, we'll confirm via email/phone. For high-value items, we may ask for a short video. The goal is to make sure the listing is accurate so buyers trust KLTUF.
What if my item doesn't pass verification? ▼
We'll let you know why. Common reasons: not enough photos, condition doesn't match description, or item type not accepted. You can resubmit with better photos or more detail.
What categories do you accept? ▼
Home & furniture, women's fashion, men's clothing, kids & toys, spices & food, habesha/cultural items, electronics, and more. We reserve the right to decline items that are prohibited, counterfeit, or unsellable.
Do I need to ship the item myself? ▼
Once your item sells, we coordinate with you. You can ship directly to the buyer (we provide the label) or arrange a local drop-off.
When do I get paid? ▼
Within 3 business days after the buyer confirms receipt. We pay via Zelle, PayPal, or Venmo — your choice.
What if my item doesn't sell? ▼
If your item hasn't sold after 30 days, we'll discuss lowering the price or returning it to you. No fees ever charged on unsold items.
